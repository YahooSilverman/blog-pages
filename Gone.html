Standing where you are

Posted by Yahoo January 11th, 2023

A quick one to fall asleep

Since this is my second website that I have all but abandoned I want to share this here. I’m going through a lot right now. I’ve written about apologies from my late father by way of a Game Boy and holes in the floor representing a close friend’s suicide, but right now I want to write about me. In my life I feel used from time to time, as I’m sure many do. I allow people to use me in my profession, I allow people to use me in my hobbies, and despite my confidence and love for myself I sometimes allow people to use me in interpersonal relationships. Maybe it’s my fear of being alone, or perhaps it’s my competitive side thinking “I will eventually make them care”.

To be honest, I’m depressed right now. I don’t want to play video games. I don’t want to make video games. I don’t want to write the code to post this online, and hell… I don’t even want to write this. I’m tired of feeling this way. I’m tired of feeling like I’m so replaceable. I’m tired of the onset anxiety as the result of someone’s carelessly selfish actions. I’m just tired. I love myself. I love my friends. I love my hobbies, yet the second I care about someone on a certain level I give them access to my emotions and sometimes they just use them as a rug to wipe their shoes off before walking through someone else’s home.

Those of you who know and support me, thank you. I WILL write more this year and WILL build more this year. I love you all, and everything is temporary but so are we.

Yahoo
